5 Morning Yoga Poses to Start Your Day

Yoga

Published: 9 Dec 2025

Introduction

Starting your day with yoga helps energize the body, calm the mind, and increase flexibility. These five simple poses can be done in just 15‚Äì20 minutes.

1. Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar)

Flow through the sequence to stretch your body and warm up your muscles.

2. Cat-Cow Stretch

Gently warms up the spine and improves posture.

3. Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Enhances balance, focus, and strengthens your legs.

4. Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

Relieves tension in the back and stretches hamstrings.

5. Corpse Pose (Shavasana)

End your session with deep relaxation and mindful breathing.

Remember, consistency is key. Start slow, listen to your body, and gradually increase duration and intensity.
